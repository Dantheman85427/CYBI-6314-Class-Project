Warranty Policy

Coverage:

  • Covers any defects in materials used to manufacture your product
  • Covers any broken components under normal use if failure is below 5 years after purchase

Remediation:

  • PC Hardware Pro will repair product, at no cost, if the the product is under warranty
  • PC Hardware Pro will repair any broken parts of a product using new or replacement parts
  • PC Hardware Pro will exchange the defective and/or broken product with a new product
  • PC Hardware Pro will refund defective product if customer desires refund instead of fix or replacement

PC Hardware Pro Warranty does not cover:

  • Damage caused by negligence, improper handling, or misuse of the product.
  • Damage resulting from unauthorized modifications, repairs, BIOS flashing, overclocking, or firmware changes performed by non-authorized technicians.
  • Damage caused by improper installation, including incorrect mounting, inadequate cooling, incompatible components, or misuse of power connectors.
  • Damage due to electrical issues, including power surges, lightning strikes, or improper power supply usage.
  • Damage caused by natural disasters such as flooding, fire, earthquakes, hurricanes, or other acts of nature.
  • Issues arising from third-party software, drivers, or operating system modifications.
  • Products that have been altered, defaced, or tampered with, including removed or damaged serial number labels.
  • Normal wear-and-tear or expected degradation over time after 5 years after time of purchase
  • Theft, loss, or misplacement of the product.
  • Damage caused by using the product outside recommended environmental conditions, such as excessive dust, humidity, heat, or poor ventilation. (list is not exhaustive)
